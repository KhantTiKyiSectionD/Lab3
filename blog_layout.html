What is a Capybara?

Capybaras are the biggest rodents in the world. They live in South America and are very friendly.

Where Do They Live?

They love being near water like rivers and lakes. Capybaras are good swimmers.

What Do They Eat?

Capybaras eat grass, plants, and sometimes fruits. They are herbivores!

Fun Facts!

  • They can weigh up to 150 pounds!
  • Capybaras are social animals.
  • They have webbed feet!
